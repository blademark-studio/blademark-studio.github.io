Blademark is an independent graphic design studio founded by Andjela & Aleksandar Pavlovic, a couple specializing in crafting unique logos and authentic visual identities for clients with taste.

Andjela

VECTOR NINJA

I love using my creativity to discover visual solutions for all types of businesses. I am most passionate about logo design, it’s the one thing that has always stuck with me throughout my professional career. My design style can best be described as minimal and clean, with personality. As you've noticed, I'm slightly obsessed with black and white, the essentials, and keeping things simple and clean. I believe that if a logo can be made to work this way first, it'll undoubtedly color the world.

Aleksandar

DIGITAL MONK

I bring logos to life, using cutting-edge animation techniques and a deep understanding of visual storytelling to create dynamic animations that capture the essence of your brand. With a background in both graphic design and animation, I'm able to bring a unique perspective to every project, fusing creativity with technical expertise to deliver animations that not only look great, but also help you connect with your audience on a deeper level.

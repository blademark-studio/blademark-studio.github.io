The Story Behind

We cover a large range of creative digital projects, platforms, and campaigns to create experiences.

Blademark™ (also known as BDMK™) is an independent graphic design studio founded by Andjela & Aleksandar Pavlovic, a couple specialized in crafting unique logos & awesome merch for brands, bussinesses and individuals with taste.

We are a freelance couple driven by love of design, passion for simplicity and fascination with the latest tech. Expressing creativity by using shapes, colour, typography, putting all the pieces together and figuring out the best ways to make things work has always been a part of our DNA.

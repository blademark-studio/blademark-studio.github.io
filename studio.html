Blademark Studio

Blademark™ (also known as BDMK™) is an independent graphic design studio founded by Andjela & Aleksandar Pavlovic, a couple specialized in crafting unique identities & experiences for brands and bussinesses with taste.

We are a freelance couple driven by love of design, passion for simplicity and fascination with the latest tech. We love crafting stuff and the whole creative process behind it. Expressing creativity by using shapes, colour, typography, putting all the pieces together and figuring out the best ways to make things work has always been a part of our DNA.
